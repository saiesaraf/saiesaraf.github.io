Viscosity Measurement
Viscosity describes fluid’s internal resistance to flow and measure of fluid friction. Monitoring and trending viscosity is perhaps one of the most important components of any oil analysis program. The viscosity sensor is fabricated using a DC motor and the optical transmitter-receiver pair. The obtained pulses are given to Schmitt trigger to remove noise and further processing is carried out using AVR microcontroller.
